Welcome to Solar Solutions!

We are a leading provider of solar energy solutions, dedicated to bringing clean and sustainable energy to our customers. Located in Germiston, we have been serving residential, commercial, and industrial clients for over 14 years.

Our mission is to help individuals and businesses reduce their carbon footprint while saving money on energy costs through the power of solar technology. At Solar Solutions, we offer a wide range of services, including solar panel installation, maintenance, and consultation.

Our team of experienced professionals is committed to delivering high-quality products and exceptional customer service. We work closely with our clients to design customised solar solutions that meet their specific energy needs and budget. We believe in the power of renewable energy to create a brighter and more sustainable future for our planet.

Our History: Helios Solar Solutions

2014: The Spark

Helios Solar Solutions was founded in a small garage in Centurion, Gauteng, by two engineers, Thabo Molefe and Sarah Jenkins. Frustrated by the increasing instability of the national power grid, they began experimenting with small-scale off-grid battery systems for local neighbourhood homes.

2017: Commercial Expansion

As South Africans faced more frequent Stage 4 load-shedding, the demand for clean, reliable energy skyrocketed. Helios moved out of the garage and into its first warehouse, shifting focus to professional commercial-grade installations — helping local schools and clinics stay powered during outages.

2021: The Green Revolution

By 2021, Helios had installed over 500 residential systems across South Africa. We partnered with leading international battery manufacturers to bring high-efficiency lithium-ion technology to the local market at an affordable price.

2026: Leading the Future

Today, Helios Solar Solutions is a leader in sustainable energy. We don't just sell panels — we provide energy independence. With a team of 50 certified installers, we are dedicated to our mission of putting a solar panel on every roof in the country.
