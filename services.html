Frequently Asked Questions

Most residential installations are completed within 1 – 2 days. Commercial and industrial installations may take 3 – 5 days depending on the size of the system. We will give you a more accurate timeline during your free site assessment.

On their own, grid-tied solar panels will not work during load-shedding for safety reasons. However, when paired with a battery backup system, your home or business can continue to run on stored solar energy even when the grid is down.

Most of our residential customers see a 60 – 90% reduction in their monthly electricity bill. The exact saving depends on your energy usage, the size of the system installed, and how much sunlight your property receives. Our consultants will calculate your expected saving before you commit to anything.

All solar panels we install come with a 25-year performance warranty from the manufacturer. Our batteries carry a 10-year warranty. Our installation workmanship is covered by a 5-year warranty from Helios Solar Solutions directly.

Solar systems are very low maintenance. We recommend a professional inspection and clean once a year to keep performance at its best. You can sign up for one of our annual maintenance packages and we will take care of everything for you.
